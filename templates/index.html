AG Outdoors | Captain Andy Gustafson



USCG Captain Andy Gustafson grew up on the shores of Lake Superior. Let his 40 years of multi-species experience on Lake Superior and surrounding Lakes help create lasting memories.

        If your interest is a private tour of the sea caves or a sunset cruise in the apostle islands, Captain Andy can make that happen.

        In between family and guiding, Captain Andy is in his sixth year of fishing the Aims Professional Walleye Tour. Due to his experience and success in competitive fishing, Captain Andy brings proven techniques to pass along to clients.

        You will be comfortable and dry while fishing out of a 2021 219 TFX Yarcraft with smooth move seats Powered by a 350 Suzuki with a 9.9 Suzuki kicker. At the helm there is two humminbird Gen 4 graphs and at the bow there is a Garmin Live Scope with a Minn Kota Terrova 112 lb I-Pilot Link.

        From family fun to the hardcore angler, your next adventure awaits!
