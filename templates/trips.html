Trips

Lake Superior and Surrounding Lakes: All Year

From chasing trophy smallmouth bass on Chequamegon Bay to open water trolling for trout and salmon and chasing big water walleye, Lake Superior has you covered. Our inland waters are a good backup when mother nature doesn't allow us on Lake Superior or if you would prefer a smaller body of water. Our inland waters offer excellent fishing for Walleye, Bass, and Panfish.

Lake Mille Lacs Trip: May and June

Offering Walleye fishing trips on Lake Mille Lacs in May and June. This trip you will find that 100 fish days are possible and your next trophy Walleye is just a cast away!

Lake of the Woods Trip: August

In the month of August I offer trips to Big Traverse Bay on the famous Lake of the Woods. You will experience limits of Walleyes with many true giants mixed in.

Apostle Islands Sunset Tours: Summer Months

Experience our seacaves, tour the Apostle Islands to take in the natural beauty that Lake Superior has to offer. Explore the Apostle Islands including their secluded sandy beaches, multiple historic lighthouses, natural wildlife, and breathtaking sunset.
